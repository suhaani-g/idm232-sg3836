How to Use Our Website

Learn how to search for recipes, use filters, and discover delicious meals!

1. Searching for Recipes

To search for your favorite recipes, simply enter keywords into the search bar located at the top of any page. You can search by ingredients, recipe names, or specific meal types.

2. Using Recipe Filters

After searching, use the filter options on the left side of the page to narrow down your results. Filter by cooking time, meal type, or cuisine to find exactly what you're looking for!

3. Exploring Recipe Categories

Not sure what you're craving? Browse through different recipe categories by clicking "All Recipes" in the menu bar. Explore by cuisine, course, or popular dishes.

4. Viewing a Recipe

Click on any recipe card to view detailed instructions, ingredients, and cooking tips. You can also save recipes to your favorites for easy access later!
