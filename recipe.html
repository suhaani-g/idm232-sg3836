Chipotle Chicken & Cilantro Rice

With Cotija Cheese & Poblano Pepper

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Poblano Pepper
  • 1/4 Cup Cilantro
  • 1/4 Cup Cotija Cheese
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 Cup White Rice
  • 2 Tbsp Chipotle Sauce

Preparation

  1. Cook the rice and set aside.
  2. Roast the poblano pepper and prepare the chicken with the chipotle sauce.
  3. Mix the rice with cilantro and lime, then serve with the cooked chicken and roasted pepper.
  4. Garnish with Cotija cheese and additional cilantro if desired.
